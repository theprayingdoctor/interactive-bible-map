    Interactive Bible Map

    Interactive Bible Map is a free ministry resource by ThePrayingDoctor designed to help people explore the Book of Acts geographically and chronologically, grounding Scripture in real places and historical context.

    Scripture

    All Scripture quotations are from the King James Version (KJV), which is in the public domain.

    Limitations & Disclaimers

    Ancient locations are approximate. Exact sites of some Biblical events remain debated by historians and archaeologists.

    Gospel chronology follows a widely-held historical reconstruction. Scholarly opinion on the precise sequence of Jesus' ministry varies.

    This resource is designed as a study aid, not a substitute for Scripture. All passages should be read in their full Biblical context.
